## Download Instructions

### For SEC Filings:
1. Go to https://www.sec.gov/cgi-bin/browse-edgar?action=getcompany&CIK=1807794
2. Select document type (10-K, 10-Q, 8-K, etc.)
3. Click on the filing date to open the filing
4. Look for ".htm" or ".pdf" version of document
5. Download and save to appropriate subfolder
6. If only .htm available, use browser "Save as PDF" function

### For Earnings Transcripts:
1. Go to https://investors.credosemi.com/
2. Find "Events & Presentations" or "Investor Resources"
3. Look for "Earnings Calls" or "Transcripts"
4. Click on desired call date
5. Download transcript (usually PDF or text)
6. Save to Earnings_Transcripts folder

### For Analyst Articles:
1. Go to https://seekingalpha.com/symbol/CRDO
2. Click on "Articles" section
3. Sort by "Most Recent" or "Most Popular"
4. Open desired article
5. Use browser "Print to PDF" or "Save as PDF" feature
6. Save to Analyst_Articles folder

## Important Notes

- **Document Access:** SEC filings are public and free; Seeking Alpha may have some premium content (premium subscription may be required for certain articles)
- **Data Freshness:** As of April 2026, the most recent data would be Q1 2026 earnings (reported in April 2026) and potentially 2025 annual report
- **Verification:** Always verify the document date and type before saving
- **Organization:** Keep folder structure organized for easy reference during analysis

## Analysis Preparation

Once all documents are collected, recommended analysis approach:
1. Start with most recent 10-K and 10-Q for baseline understanding
2. Review earnings transcripts in chronological order to understand business trajectory
3. Read analyst articles to understand key debates and investment theses
4. Extract key financial metrics, growth rates, and competitive advantages
5. Cross-reference across documents for consistency
